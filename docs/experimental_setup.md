# Experimental Setup

## Objective

The experimental system was designed to generate frequency-response measurements for a non-ideal series RLC circuit.

The measurements provide the data used for parameter reconstruction and identifiability analysis.

---

## Circuit Topology

Signal Generator → Inductor → Capacitor → Resistor → Ground

The output measurement was taken across the resistor.

---

## Components

Nominal values:

* Inductor: 10 mH
* Capacitor: 220 nF

Resistance values investigated:

* 22 Ω
* 47 Ω
* 100 Ω
* 150 Ω
* 200 Ω
* 270 Ω
* 330 Ω
* 470 Ω

---

## Equipment

* FY6900 Signal Generator
* FNIRSI 5012H Oscilloscope
* Breadboard
* Assorted resistors
* 10 mH inductor
* 220 nF polyester capacitor

---

## Measurements

For each frequency:

* Source voltage (Vs)
* Resistor voltage (VR)

were recorded.

Gain was calculated as:

Gain = VR / Vs

Frequency sweeps were performed over the resonance region to capture the full frequency-response curve.
